The Outlaw

Arthur Morgan is the lead enforcer of the Van der Linde gang. Raised by Dutch van der Linde and Hosea Matthews, he is a man torn between the brutal world of the Wild West and the shifting tides of civilization.

"We're thieves in a world that don't want us no more."

The Brotherhood

Loyalty was everything. For Arthur, the gang wasn't just a group of criminals; it was his only family. He carried the weight of their survival on his shoulders.

"I gave you all I had. I did."
